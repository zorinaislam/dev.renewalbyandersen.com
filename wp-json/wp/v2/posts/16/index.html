While internet service providers typically offer router and modem setups, opting for your own router can often lead to enhanced home network performance and coverage. With numerous options flooding the market, some routers stand out as top contenders for 2024.<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. TP-Link Archer AX21<\/strong><\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    Priced under $100, the TP-Link Archer AX21 delivers affordability and supports speeds of up to Gigabit. Featuring EasyMesh, a user-friendly mesh network setup, this router competes admirably with more expensive alternatives without compromising on performance. With full Wi-Fi 6 support and compatibility with Alexa, it offers speeds of up to 1.8 Gbps and a four-antenna system to extend coverage throughout your home. For a reliable router that won’t necessitate an upgrade for years to come, TP-Link is a solid starting point.<\/p>\n

      \n
    1. Synology WRX560<\/strong><\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

      The Synology WRX560 comes at a higher price point than the TP-Link but justifiably so, as it boasts full dual-band support for Wi-Fi 6 and the potential for internet connections of up to 2.5 Gbps. This router is essentially future-proof, ready to handle high-speed internet when available. Notably, it supports multiple network IDs, enabling specific configurations for computers, gaming consoles, or smartphones. It also includes built-in parental controls, a VPN system, and seamless integration into a mesh system for comprehensive home coverage, making it a recommendation from PCMag.<\/p>\n

        \n
      1. Netgear Orbi RBK852 Mesh Router<\/strong><\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

        For large homes or offices requiring extensive Wi-Fi coverage, a mesh router is an ideal choice. Mesh routers combine the power of two systems to expand coverage without compromising speed or connectivity. The Netgear Orbi RBK852 excels in this regard, as it includes both a router and a satellite extender. This setup can cover up to 5,000 square feet and connect up to 100 devices, ensuring all your smart devices have internet access. While it comes at a higher price point, the support for speeds up to 6 Gbps ensures your home network remains future-proof.<\/p>\n

          \n
        1. ASUS ROG Rapture GT-AX11000 Gaming Router<\/strong><\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

          If gaming takes center stage in your internet activities, investing in a gaming router can significantly enhance your experience. The ASUS ROG Rapture GT-AX11000 is among the best options, tailored specifically for gamers. It features triple-level game acceleration, network security without subscription fees, and support for speeds up to 10 Gbps and 2.5 Gbps, depending on the port you use. The router’s internals are designed with gaming in mind, making tasks like port forwarding and internet connection customization seamless. While it comes at a higher cost, the ASUS ROG Rapture GT-AX11000 is highly praised for its performance and features by PCMag.<\/p>\n
